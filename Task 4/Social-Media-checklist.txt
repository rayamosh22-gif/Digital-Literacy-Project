Social Media Do’s and Dont’s for College Students
Do’s:
1. Think before posting — consider long-term impact.
2. Maintain a professional tone on public platforms.
3. Verify information before sharing.
4. Respect others’ privacy and opinions.
5. Use social media for networking and learning opportunities.
Don’ts:
1. Don’t share offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
2. Don’t overshare personal or sensitive information.
3. Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling.
4. Don’t post misleading or fake information.
5. Don’t ignore digital footprints — what you post stays online.
