                                             “Stay Safe Online” Checklist

Do these unless you enjoy donating money to strangers online:
General Safety Tips
1. Never click on suspicious links in emails or messages. 
2. Always verify the sender’s email address or phone number. 
3. Use strong, unique passwords for each account. 
4. Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible. 
5. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions. 
6. Keep your devices and apps updated regularly. 
UPI / Financial Safety (Important, please don’t ignore this part)
7. Never share OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone. 
8. Do not approve unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps. 
Reporting Channels (India specific)
* National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in 
* Helpline Number: 1930
